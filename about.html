Welcome to JG FLAT FIX!

JG Flat Fix is a reputable, family-owned tire shop at the heart of the Village of Hempstead. As a trusted establishment, we take pride in offering a comprehensive range of tire-related services to cater to the diverse needs of our valued customers. Specializing in tire sales, our shop provides a wide variety of tires suitable for different vehicles, including cars, trucks, SUVs, motorcycles, and more. Our experienced team excels in tire installation, ensuring precise alignment and balancing for optimal performance. We prioritize tire longevity through services such as tire rotation, promoting even wear on all four tires.

At JG Flat Fix, we understand the importance of wheel balancing, guaranteeing the even distribution of weight to prevent vibrations and uneven tire wear. Our expertise extends to flat tire repair, addressing punctures and flats with meticulous attention, either through patching or plugging the tire. Safety and fuel efficiency are paramount, and our tire pressure check services ensure that your vehicle maintains the correct tire pressure.

Committed to your safety on the road, we conduct professional tire inspections, identifying wear, damage, and potential issues to ensure both safety and performance. Our tire maintenance services go beyond the basics, offering tire cleaning, sidewall treatment, and tire conditioning for a comprehensive care package. Furthermore, we are environmentally conscious, ensuring proper tire recycling to dispose of old or worn-out tires responsibly.

For those seeking customization options, JG Flat Fix provides custom wheel and tire packages, allowing customers to personalize their vehicles with unique combinations. Additionally, our shop extends its services to wheel repair, addressing damages or bends in wheels to keep your entire wheel assembly in top-notch condition. Choose JG Flat Fix for a family-owned tire shop experience that combines years of expertise with a commitment to quality service.
